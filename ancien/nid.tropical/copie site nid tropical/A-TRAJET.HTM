ROUTE

From the airport go on the left to BASSE-TERRE. At 2nd circus go on the right to BASSE-TERRE.

5 Kms turn to direction "BOUILLANTE/POINTE- NOIRE ROUTE DE LA TRAVERSEE". At circus same direction. Drive by "COL DES MAMELLES" and when you arrive at the sea, turn on the left to "BOUILLANTE". At 5 km you cross the Malendure beach, 1 km after you climb the road before SHELL gas station. 300 m after on the parking you see "NID TROPICAL".

Route about 45 to 50 mn / 40 kms
