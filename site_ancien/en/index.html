Le Nid Tropical

Sylvie and Jean Pierre, owner of Tropical Nest, welcome you in their Creole bungalows completely refurbished and re-equipped and in their apartment. You will spend unforgettable holidays in Guadeloupe, in Bouillante in a paradise with one of the most beautiful views on the Caribbean islands and Pigeon Bay Malendure in a quiet residential district.

The Nest Tropical accompany you during walks in the Caribbean on their catamaran "Loubine and offers diving trips at preferential rates, diving to Malendure islets Pigeon, the famous Cousteau reserve in Guadeloupe and a free introductory pool with its partner the Center islets.

If you're looking for adventure, hiking, canyoning and exotic scenery to admire, choose a stay in the Caribbean! in Guadeloupe on the Lower Earth Nest Tropical!

Check out our rental offers Creole bungalows with fan and air-conditioned Bouillante in an exotic setting over the bay Malendure. Sylvie and Jean Pierre offer:

- 6 Bungalows with fan and air conditioning option 3 people

- 4 air-conditioned bungalows for 4 persons

- 1 apartment for 4, 6 or 9 people with fan and air conditioning, consisting of 2 bedrooms + 2 bathrooms with toilet and a large kitchen comfortable terrace. New! Possibility of a 3rd bedroom and more.

All our bungalows, apartment, carbet and swimming pool, enjoy the unique sea view! More! You have a free wireless internet access. If you do not bring your laptop we offer access to office according to our convenience.
